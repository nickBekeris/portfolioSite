About Me

There are many activities I enjoy to participate in, but I wont bore you that long list right now. However, I have been developing and teaching myself many languages for just under a decade, and using these skills for others now as well.

Development has definitely become a passion of mine, and I plan to make a success from what I am continuously learning. I currently host servers for a few different video games (counter-strike & minecraft), as well as some voIP servers (mumble), and development sandboxes.

I also run a blog where I plan to keep updates of new projects as well as useful information that I continue to learn.

Technical Experience

I have many different levels experience with a variety of different languages; however, recently I have been using Node.JS with different front and back end JavaScript libraries. I have been using the MEAN stack mostly with the new applications and sites in my portfolio, but LAMP stacks are an art I have come close to mastering as well.

I first started with HTML, CSS, & JavaScript back in 2007 to develop a simple website on a LAMP stack for a gaming group I played with and admired. Ever since then I have been advancing further into OOP and MVC development using PHP, and now AngluarJS on MEAN stacks.

I have quickly fallen in love with AngularJS, BackboneJS, and KnockoutJS for creating different single page applications. This website here is running off of a MEAN stack with AngularJS and jQuery. You can view some more of the applications and languages I've used in my portfolio.
